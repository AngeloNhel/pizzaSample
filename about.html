About Us

At 360° Degrees, customer satisfaction is our top priority. We strive to provide exceptional service, from the moment guests walk through our doors to the last bite of their meal. Our friendly staff is dedicated to exceeding expectations and creating a warm, inviting atmosphere for diners of all ages.

Located in the heart of Leyte, 360° Degrees’ benefits from high foot traffic and visibility, making it a prime destination for locals, tourists, and families alike. Our convenient and accessible location ensures that customers can enjoy our delicious pizzas with ease.
